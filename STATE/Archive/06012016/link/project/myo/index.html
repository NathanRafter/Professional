Project summary

MyO, the name of which comes from My Orlando, is an application designed to aggregate events, points-of-interest, and restaurants from the many fragmented sources available in Central Florida. It allows users to easily find activities and invite their friends along. I created it so that my wife and I could discover and embark on more adventures together.


Mobile

The simple interface is focused on drawing the user's attention to a series of call to actions that gradually establish more specific search criteria and help MyO pin-point the perfect option.
